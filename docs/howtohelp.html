There are several ways how tou can support our initiative and help Ukrainian population in Ukraine. Every guesture matters, every dollar counts, every initiative is important, every effort can save someone's live!

Volunteering

Come help us in one of our Donations collect centers. The task may include answering the phone calls and emails, welcoming people and receiving donations, sorting and packing the goods into the boxes, preparing the pallets for shipping, loading/unloading the trucks, other coordination as needed.
