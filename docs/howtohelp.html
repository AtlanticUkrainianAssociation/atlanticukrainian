There are several ways how you can support our initiative and help Ukrainian population in Ukraine. Every guesture matters, every dollar counts, every initiative is important, every effort can save someone's live!

VOLUNTEERING

Come help us in one of our locations. The task may include answering the phone calls and emails, welcoming people and receiving donations, sorting and packing the goods into the boxes, preparing the pallets for shipping, loading/unloading the trucks, other coordination as needed.

Become a volunteer

GOODS DONATION

Whether you are a regular person, store, restaurant, pharmacy, or other business, your donation could save lives.

Things we collect and send over to Ukraine:

  • Medication and medical supplies (not open, new, not expired)
  • Survival products
  • Baby products (new only)
  • Hygienic products
  • Dry food (does not require cooking)

MONETARY DONATION

We are a purely volunteer-based organization, and every donation supports our operation. Donate today to help get life-saving items and food to Ukrainian families who need them the most. It takes a minute to donate, but the impact can last a lifetime.
